Adventuring Options#

Rest Variant#

This campaign follows the rules for the Gritty Realism Rest Variant from the Dungeon Master's Guide.

Combat Options#

Injuries#

We will occasionally use the Lingering Injuries alternate rule.

Massive Damage#

We will occasionally use the Massive Damage alternate rule.

Magic#

Due to the unhinged nature of the weave after the Starfire Calamity, we use Dangerous Magic rules for spellcasting.
